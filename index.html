Hossein (Maziar) Aqasizade

I am a PhD student at the University of Trento, collaborating with Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK) through its OpenIoT unit, where I focus on Edge Computing, IoT, and MLOps innovations under the advisorship of Professor Massimo Vecchio.

My research interests lie in cloud computing, serverless computing, and IoT. In 2023, I completed my MSc in Computer Engineering at the University of Mazandaran, where my thesis focused on enhancing Kubernetes cluster and serverless platform performance by integrating virtual machines and containers. I also hold a BSc in Computer Engineering from Islamic Azad University (2019).
